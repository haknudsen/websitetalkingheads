Kickstarter is a funding platform for creative projects. Everything from films, games, and music to art, design, and technology. Ambitious, innovative, and imaginative ideas flock to Kickstarter. Then Kickstarter brings those ideas to life through the direct support of others.

Everything on Kickstarter must be a project with a clear goal, like making an album, a book, or a work of art. When a project is completed, there is a product.

Definitions

Project
A project is a finite work with a clear goal that you’d like to bring to life. Think albums, books, or films. The funding goal is the amount of money that a creator needs to complete their project.
Funding
Kickstarter uses all-or-nothing funding. Backers are not billed until the project reaches the funding goal. This way, creators always have the budget they scoped out before moving forward.
Creator
The person or team behind the project idea is call the Creator. They are the ones working to bring the project to life.
Backers
Backers are folks who pledge money to join creators in bringing projects to life.
Rewards
Rewards are a creator's chance to share a piece of their project with their backer community. Typically, these are one-of-a-kind experiences, limited editions, or copies of the product.
