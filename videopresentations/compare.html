Video Presentations

Depending on the package you purchace, you may choose a mix of video, text, images, and music. There are also many graphical effects and transitions to help your Video Presentation stand out. You can create a video wtih text layered over the dialog where the text will appear when the words are spoken, empasizing the feelings behind the words.

  • All of our Video Presentations can contain Motion Background, Logo, Phone Number, website URL, and one of our Spokespeople.
  • We have many Motion Backgrounds for you to choose from. You can see previews of them here. Each of these can be customised for your Video Presentation. We can also create a unique Motion Background if needed.
  • Our Intros and Outros are 3 to 8 seconds long and can include your logo, phone number and website as well as animation and particle effects.
  • There is a limit on the Total Visuals depending on your package. This is the total number of Text, Image and Video Elements in your Video Presentation. You can have a combination of elements up to the maximum in a category. So with an Executive Package you could have 10 text elements, 10 images, and 10 videos. Or you could have 20 text elements and 10 images.
  • Music can be a very important aspect of a Video Presentation. Music helps set the mood and tone of a presentation. All music used must be royalty free or owned by you.
  • We can add sound effects to emphasise a visual image or the point you are trying to make. Sound effects must be royalty free or owned by you.
  • Total visuals include any text, images, videos or screen captures. Everything in the video must be royalty free or owned by you.
  • Text is used to emphasise what the video spokesperson is saying and can really help you get your point across.
  • Images are an important way to get your point across and help tell your story. Images must be royalty free or owned by you.
  • Video can be added to your own Video Presentation to give it a bit of flare. Video must be royalty free or owned by you.
  • We can have Screen Captures of your website in your video. You can use them to guide visitors around your site.
  • Typography is a special kind of video where most, if not all of the words the spokesperson says are also on the screen.
  • Animation is a new aspect of videos that we enjoy putting into videos. Ask us about the Animation we can do for you.
  • Due to the low cost at which we provide our Video Presentations, the revision process is very important. Make sure that when you go over the storyboard and script with us that everything is there and presented the way that you want it to be.
